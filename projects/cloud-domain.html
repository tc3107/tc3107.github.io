Case Study

Cloud, DNS & Domain Operations

A case study on using hosted systems and domain infrastructure to support dependable operations, secure access, and maintainable service administration.

Context

Small-business environments often depend on a mix of local infrastructure and hosted services. DNS, domains, cloud systems, and third-party providers become part of the operational surface and need the same care as on-premise systems.

Problem / Goal

The goal is to keep hosted services reachable, manageable, and appropriately secured while avoiding needless complexity and preserving clear ownership of operational changes.

My Role

I use Google Cloud, Hetzner, Cloudflare, DNS, domains, and virtual or cloud systems to support operations. That includes the practical administration needed to connect hosted services with internal systems and user-facing workflows.

What I Built or Maintained

  • DNS and domain administration required for reliable service access and controlled changes.
  • Hosted or virtual system management supporting business operations and remote availability.
  • Cloudflare-related configuration for access, DNS, and service exposure management.
  • Cross-system troubleshooting where local infrastructure and hosted services interact.

Tools / Technologies

Google Cloud, Hetzner, Cloudflare, DNS, domain management, Linux-based hosted systems, and operational documentation.

Security / Reliability Considerations

This page does not expose domain names, provider-specific account details, private configuration, or traffic patterns. The relevant public takeaway is that cloud and domain work is handled with change awareness, access discipline, and a focus on dependable service reachability.

What This Demonstrates to Employers

I can work across the boundary between local systems and hosted infrastructure. That includes DNS reasoning, provider administration, debugging access issues, and keeping cloud decisions tied to practical operational needs rather than novelty.

Skills Demonstrated

Google Cloud Hetzner Cloudflare DNS Domains Hosted systems Access management Troubleshooting Documentation
